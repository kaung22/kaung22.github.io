PlayerProfile
Lionel Andres Messi, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Seint Germain and captain of the Argentina national team. He was born on 24 June 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. His parents are Jorge Horacio Messi(was a factory worker) and Celia Maria Cuccittini(was a part-time cleaner.) He has two older brothers Rodrigo and Matias and a sister Maria Sol.
  • At the age of five, Messi started playing football for Grandoli, a local club coached by his father Jorge.
  • He was diagnosed with a growth deficiency at 11, requiring growth hormone injections.
  • His debut with the Argentinian national team (against Hungary) was a diaster : he entered thegame in the 18th minute of the second half, and 47 seconds later was sent off.
  • He made his La Liga debut for Barcelona against Espanyol when he was 17 years old.
